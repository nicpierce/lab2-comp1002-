How to Make Grilled Cheese

Ingredients you'll need:

    • - 2 pieces of bread
    • - 2 pats of butter
    • - 1 slice of monterey jack cheese
    • - 1 slice of aged cheddar cheese

Process:

    • 1. Fetch yourself a fat-bottom skillet
    • 2. Heat the skillet over medium heat
    • 3. Butter one side of each slice of bread
    • 4. Place one slice of the buttered bread, buttered side down, into the skillet
    • 5. Quickly place each slice of cheese on the bread, being sure to cover the entire surface area
    • 6. Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese
    • 7. Wait approximately 1 to 2 minutes (or till the bread touching the skillet is a golden brown)
    • 8. Flip the sandwich over with some awesome pizazz and cook the other side till golden brown (approximately 1 to 2 minutes)
    • 9. Eat with ketchup, or just enjoy the plain crispy goodness of your grilled cheese

      • mmm,mmm, good!


      Note:
      Grilled cheese is great on a grill, but for a quicker method, try using a clamshell panini press. The result will be much quicker, allowing you to make many more for you and your friends. Remember, Shaun says "Grilled cheese sandwiches are a staple of the North American diet!".
